Ferrari Roma

The Ferrari Roma, which was unveiled back in November 2019, has finally arrived in India, with the company announcing its prices in the country. The 2+ Coupe, as the brand likes to call it, pays homage to the life in the city of Rome back in the 1950s and 60s. the hood of the Ferrari Roma is the award-winning 3.9-litre turbocharged V8 engine that produces 612bhp and 760Nm of torque. Mated to an eight-speed DCT unit, the model can sprint from 0-100kmph in 3.4 seconds, followed by 200kmph in 9.3 seconds. The top speed of the model stands at 320kmph.
